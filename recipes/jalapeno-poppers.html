Jalapeno Poppers

Description

These jalape√±o poppers are creamy in the middle with crispy bacon on the outside. They're easy to make and taste so much better than traditional poppers.

Ingredients

  • Cream cheese

  • Jalapenos

  • Bacon

Steps

  1. Make the filling.

  2. Fill each jalapeno half with cheese mixture, then put the halves back together.

  3. Wrap each reassembled jalapeno with bacon.

  4. Cook in the preheated oven until the bacon is crispy.
